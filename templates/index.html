Holland's RIASEC Model

Maps your interests across six types — Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, Conventional — inspired by the Strong Interest Inventory.

Big Five Personality (OCEAN)

Evaluates Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism through scenario-based questions, weighted 1.5x with text analysis.

Multi-Aptitude Battery

Tests logical reasoning (pattern sequences, deduction), creative thinking (open innovation challenges), and verbal comprehension.

Rule-Based Text Analysis

Analyzes your open-text responses for personality markers through keyword extraction, sentence patterns, and contradiction detection.

CSS Scoring Model

Career Suitability Score: 30% interest match + 25% aptitude + 25% traits + 10% behavioral + 10% stage fit. Scored 0–100 with ≥70 threshold.
